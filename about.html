Hey, I'm Kenny!

I'm a Product Designer with experience in web design and development. I graduated from Queens College in New York City with a Bachelors in Design. I started off as a web developer, but as time went on, I realized I resonated with design much more. I am an avid fan of minimalistic, clean, and user-centric designs.

I started off setting my eyes on becoming a front end web developer. Unfortunately, I found out that Queens College did not support any courses on web development. This made me lean towards the next useful course - design. I already had some artistic learning experiences at high school, so I wanted to dive more into it in college. In the meantime, I improved my web development skills on the side by learning it whenever I had free time.

I eventually began working as a freelancer and full-time web developer with design responsibilities. As time went on, I began to realize my passion and skills were more in sync with designing - in particular UX and UI. Thus, I began to focus most of my attention on those skills.

Thanks for visiting!
